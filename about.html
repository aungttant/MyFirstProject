Our Story

Unique Furniture Co.Ltd established in Yangon since 2000 is the professional manufacturing and distributing company of spring mattress, bedding accessories and modern wood furniture. Now many factories have been erected in Yangon to produce and to distribute all over myanmar. Applying the high technology and using premium quality raw materials, we produce our brands “ Unique Furniture”, “ Pwint Oo ”, “ Sein Mya ” and so on.

Generation

In 2015 as second generation, we import steel, aluminium and sofa from Thailand and Korea to distribute through own sale and retail outlets. In 2017 as third generation, we import high standard plastic furniture form foreign company.
